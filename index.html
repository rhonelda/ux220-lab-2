The best thing i read

i read an article on Canada's Housing and Rental Issues In major cities like Toronto, Vancouver, and even some smaller urban centers, housing prices had surged to record highs. This made homeownership increasingly unaffordable for many Canadians, particularly first-time buyers.

The Canadian government, both at the federal and provincial levels, introduced measures to address housing affordability. These measures included tightening mortgage rules, introducing foreign buyer taxes, and implementing stricter lending criteria.It's crucial to recognize that the situation with housing and rental issues in Canada can change rapidly due to market dynamics, government policies, and external factors.
